About Me

My name is Andrew Byerly, and I've been doing web design and development as a hobby since I was 11 years old. I taught myself HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and PHP, and used them for various freelance and personal projects over the years. I studied Mechanical Engineering at Bradley University, and in 2015 I started an on-demand delivery service called All Things Delivered. While building the website and tools for the business, I realized I wanted to pursue web development as a career. I attended a full-time .Net development program at Coding Temple in 2017, and now I'm looking to start my career as a .Net/PHP developer.
